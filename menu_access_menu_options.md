**Title:**

**Acceptance Criteria:**  
1. Menu is accessible from all pages.  
2. Menu includes: Habits, Reports, Profile, Sign Out.  
3. Each menu item redirects correctly.

**Priority:** High  
**Story Points:** 2  

**Notes:**
